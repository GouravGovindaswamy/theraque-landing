Our Mission and Vision

The mission of Theraque is to provide access to care that is accessible, affordable, effectively changing the lives of patients and redefining the healthcare system with a vision to eliminate long-held societal beliefs on what healthcare is and the isolation of mental illness to overall quality of life.

A to Z in Mental Health

Theraque is the only robust holistic care model in the world operating in a telehealth setting.Theraque is bringing evidence-based biopsychosocial practices to the forefront and emphasizing the importance of integrative mental wellness. As a Theraque provider, you will be granted access to our distinctive internal referral system. You will have access to patient advocates who are properly trained to assess the health and social needs of your patients, a wide array of mental health providers, nutritionists, and consulting specialists..

At theraque, we are becoming increasingly involved in the community and supporting the positive development of our children. We can treat a whole patient, but we can’t change society without ensuring future generations of children are properly cared for as well. We will be sharing our partner Erin Royer’s platform and educational resources focused on Erikson’s theory of ages and stages of development to ensure we can complete our mission to create a healthy, thriving next generation.

How we work

The focus of Theraque is our patient centered model. To achieve this, we have created a holistic care model that addresses the needs of patients, and eliminates siloed health treatments to improve the overall health of our patients. Through the Theraque platform, we offer bespoke evidence-based practice tailored to the specific needs of the patient.
